Product designer. Film Maker. Photographer.

I am David Ukauwa, the Commissioner of Design. My passion lies in creating user-centric designs that blend creativity with technical expertise. I thrive on pushing the boundaries of innovation and delivering excellence in every project.

My expertise spans user experience, visual design, photography, and filmmaking. I believe great design stems from empathy and understanding human needs. Through my work, I aim to tell stories that inspire and connect emotionally.

Where I’m Headed...

My journey as a designer is ever-evolving, and I’m constantly striving to reach new heights in my career. I envision a future where my skills and creativity are at the forefront of groundbreaking projects that make a meaningful impact. I’m working towards leading innovative design teams, exploring cutting-edge technologies, and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in product design.

‍

I aspire to create designs that not only meet user needs but also inspire and delight. My goal is to merge functionality with beauty, ensuring every project I touch leaves a lasting impression.
